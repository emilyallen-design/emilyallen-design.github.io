WESTJET · PRODUCT DESIGN

Designing a modern in‑flight entertainment ecosystem.

Reimagining entertainment, connectivity, discovery, and engagement for passengers in low‑bandwidth flight environments.

Overview

Creating a premium digital travel experience.

This concept explored how streaming-inspired UX patterns could elevate the WestJet in‑flight experience while respecting airline constraints such as connectivity, accessibility, and cabin context.

Strategy

Borrowing the best patterns from streaming platforms.

The experience benchmarked entertainment leaders like Netflix, Disney+, and Apple TV while evaluating existing airline in‑flight ecosystems across onboarding, browsing, playback, and accessibility.
